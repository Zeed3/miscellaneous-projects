The full name of BINUS University is Universitas Bina Nusantara. The master's programs offered by BINUS University include Magister Teknik Informatika, Magister Manajemen Sistem Informasi, Magister Industrial Engineering, and Magister Manajemen/Sekolah Bisnis. The dual degree master's programs offered by BINUS University include Master of Commerce in Applied Finance, Master of Commerce in International Business, and Master of Commerce in Marketing. The doctoral programs offered by BINUS University include Doctor of Computer Science (DCS) and Doctoral Research in Management (DRM). The international undergraduate programs offered by BINUS University include Accounting, Communication, Computer Science, Fashion Design, Fashion Management, Film, Graphic and New Media, Hospitality and Tourism Management, Information System, International Business, Marketing, Automotive & Robotics Engineering, and Product Design Engineering. The online undergraduate programs offered by BINUS University include Akuntansi (D3 Lanjutan-S1), Manajemen Pemasaran (D3 Lanjutan-S1), Manajemen Pemasaran (S1), Sistem Informasi (D3 Lanjutan-S1), and Teknik Informatika (S1). The Master Track programs offered by BINUS University include Master Track MTI and Master Track Magister Manajemen Sistem Informasi. BINUS University has appointed more than 30 professors by 2024. The head of the BINUS University Board of Professors is Harjanto Prabowo.
